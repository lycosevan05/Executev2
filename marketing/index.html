FAQ

Questions, answered

Is Execute free to use?

Yes. Core manual tracking — nutrition, macros, barcode scanning, workout logging, and daily check-ins — is free. Premium unlocks AI training plans, photo macro estimation, and personalized guidance.

How does photo meal tracking work?

Tap to log a meal and snap a photo. Execute estimates the meal's macros in seconds so you can log without weighing or searching a database. You can always adjust the result.

What makes the workouts "adaptive"?

Execute builds plans around your goals and equipment, then adjusts based on your progress and daily readiness — so you train hard when you're recovered and back off when you're not.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Premium is an auto-renewing subscription you manage through your Apple ID. Cancel anytime in Settings → your name → Subscriptions, and you keep access until the end of the period.
